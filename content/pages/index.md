# Breathe better. Perform better.

A five-minute reset for high performers — calm nervous system, sharper focus, zero fluff.

Halio guides short, science-backed breathwork rounds that fit between meetings. iPhone-only at launch for a tight, polished flow. Open, breathe, reset — then get back to it with that quiet edge.

## Search in App store to download

(ios Only) Search Halio in the App Store, verify it's the correct developer and you're in.

## Key Benefits

Built for busy brains and ambitious days.

### 5-minute resets
**Inhale, exhale, hold, release**
Short guided rounds to clear noise and get you back to work with a steadier pulse.

### Immersive backdrops
**Swipe to change scenes**
Forest, ocean, night sky — soothing loops that make a desk feel like open air.

### Adaptive cues
**Audio + gentle haptics**
Light guidance for inhale/exhale timing. Or go silent — your call.

### Your rhythm
**Slow to fast modes**
Tune pace, rounds, and holds. Halio remembers what works for you.
